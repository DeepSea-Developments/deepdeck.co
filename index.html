    DeepDeck

    DeepDeck is an OpenSource, MacroPad (mini mechanical keyboard) that enables you to get more productive by using it with your work/hobby/leisure programs..

    Buy Now Learn more

    DeepDeck Features ¶

    • Open Source

      Hardware, firmware, software. Everything around DeepDeck is open source! We want you to create new things around it, new features, connect it to senors, etc!

    • Just connect it with Bluetooth

      Control your laptop, no matter if it's windows, Mac, or linux. Control your smartphone (android or iOS), you could even connect it to your TV (if it has bluetooth connection).

    • OS independent

      You can program it using an internal webpage that the DeepDeck generates. No matter if it is Windows, linux, or your smartphone, you can easily reprogram it

    • Includes wifi

      DeepDeck can receive and send information directly to the internet. Check your calendar in google, or log a status in Notion!
